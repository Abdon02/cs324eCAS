This game will have a character that will be catching falling items from the sky. The character will move left and right. Different shapes of items will be falling from the sky and each item will be worth differently. Depending on the final score, the player will get either 1, 2 or 3 stars and the game will be over. This game will have the following functionalities: Pause, Resart, Quit, and Resume.

Abdon will be responsible of creating the falling items from the sky. The items that will be falling from the sky will include the following: circles, triangles and squares. The circles will be worth 1 point, the triagles will be deduct 1 point, and the squares will be worth 2 points.
